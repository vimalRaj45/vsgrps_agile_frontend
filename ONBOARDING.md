# 🚀 Welcome to Sprintora

Welcome to **Sprintora**, your new AI-powered mission control for Agile collaboration. Whether you're a fast-growing startup or a specialized development team, Sprintora is designed to remove the friction from project management, allowing you to focus on what matters most: **building great things.**

Sprintora combines intuitive task tracking with smart AI insights to help you stay ahead of deadlines and keep your team in sync. Let’s get you up and running in under 10 minutes.

---

## 🧭 Getting Started

### 1. Sign Up & Login
Create your account using your professional email. Once registered, check your inbox for a verification link to activate your secure workspace.

### 2. Create Your Workspace
The first time you log in, you'll be prompted to set up your **Organization**. This serves as the umbrella for all your projects and team members. 

### 3. Invite Your Team
Collaboration is at the heart of Sprintora. Head to the **Users** section to invite your teammates. They’ll receive an email invitation to join your specific organization.

> [!TIP]
> 💡 **Tip:** Assign roles (Admin or Developer) carefully. Admins can manage team settings and billing, while Developers focus on project execution.

---

## 🏗️ Setting Up Your First Project

Projects are where the magic happens. A project can be a software release, a marketing campaign, or a client deliverable.

1.  **Click "Create Project":** Give it a clear name and a brief description.
2.  **Define Goals:** Set the primary objective so everyone knows what success looks like.
3.  **Add Members:** Select the team members who will be contributing to this specific project.
4.  **Set Status:** Keep stakeholders informed by updating the project status (e.g., Planning, In Progress, or On Hold).

---

## ✅ Managing Tasks & Workflow

Break your project down into actionable items.

-   **Create Tasks:** Navigate to your project and add tasks. Use clear, action-oriented titles.
-   **Assign Ownership:** Every task needs a champion. Assign a user to ensure accountability.
-   **Prioritize:** Use the **Low, Medium, or High** priority flags to help your team focus on the most critical work.
-   **Deadlines:** Set due dates to keep the momentum going.

> [!NOTE]
> ⚠️ **Note:** Tasks can be moved across stages (To Do, In Progress, Done) to provide a real-time visual of your team's velocity.

---

## 🤖 Using AI Features

Sprintora isn't just a list—it's an intelligent assistant.

-   **Smart Prioritization:** Our AI analyzes your deadlines and workload to suggest which tasks you should tackle first.
-   **Productivity Insights:** Visit your Dashboard to see smart summaries of your team's performance and bottleneck alerts.
-   **Predictive Deadlines:** The system learns from your team's pace to give you realistic estimates on when a project will be completed.

---

## 🔔 Notifications & Updates

Stay in the loop without the noise.

-   **Real-time Alerts:** The bell icon in the top bar highlights new assignments, mentions, and deadline reminders.
-   **Push Notifications:** Enable browser notifications to receive updates even when you don't have the Sprintora tab open.
-   **Email Digests:** Important updates and weekly summaries are sent directly to your inbox.

> [!TIP]
> 💡 **Tip:** You can customize your notification preferences in **Settings** to ensure you only get the alerts you care about.

---

## 📂 File & Resource Management

Keep all your project assets in one place.

-   **Seamless Uploads:** Drag and drop files directly into tasks or meetings. We support everything from design assets to technical docs.
-   **Shared Links:** Add external resources (like Google Docs or Figma files) to keep all context within the project hub.
-   **Centralized Library:** Access the **Files** section for a bird's-eye view of every asset shared across your organization.

---

## 👤 Account & Security

Your data is protected by industry-standard encryption and secure session handling.

-   **Profile Management:** Update your avatar and contact details in **Settings**.
-   **Secure Sessions:** For your protection, sessions are set to expire after 24 hours of inactivity. If you're on a private device, use the "Remember Me" option during login to stay signed in for 30 days.
-   **Easy Logout:** Always log out if you are using a shared or public computer to keep your organization's data safe.

## ⚡ Pro Tips for Best Use

-   **Batch Your Tasks:** Take 10 minutes every Monday morning to plan your week and set priorities.
-   **Use Labels:** Categorize tasks by "Bug," "Feature," or "Research" to make filtering easier.
-   **Centralize Communication:** Use task comments instead of external chat apps to keep the history of decisions tied to the work itself.

---

## ❓ Troubleshooting

### Login/Session Expired
If you find yourself logged out unexpectedly, it's usually because your 24-hour session has ended. Simply log back in to resume your work.

### Notifications Not Updating
Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If the red badge doesn't update, a quick page refresh will synchronize your latest alerts.

### File Upload Issues
We support files up to 50MB. If your upload fails, check that the file type is supported and that your organization hasn't reached its storage limit.
